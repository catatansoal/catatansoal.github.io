Eligibility Criteria: Who Can Avail the Service?

So, who exactly can avail of this fantastic free bus service? The eligibility criteria are pretty straightforward. The service is primarily for women who are residents of Punjab. This means you'll need to have some proof of residency, like an Aadhaar card, voter ID, or any other government-issued ID that confirms your address in Punjab. There's no age limit, so whether you're a young student or a senior citizen, you're eligible. The service is available on all non-AC buses operated by Punjab Roadways Transport Corporation (PRTC) and Punjab State Bus Stand Management Company (Punjab Roadways). It's important to note that this service is exclusively for women, ensuring they have a safe and comfortable travel option. There are no income-based criteria, which means women from all economic backgrounds can benefit from this scheme. The aim is to make transportation accessible to all women in Punjab, fostering inclusivity and empowerment. By keeping the eligibility criteria simple and inclusive, the government aims to maximize the reach and impact of this initiative.
