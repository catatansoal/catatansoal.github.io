🚀 Phase 3開始: パイロット実行 (Pilot Execution Start)

As part of the execution phase for Issue #8, we're kicking off the 100-school collection test in the Kanto region. 📋 実行計画 (Execution Plan)

Let's dive into the nitty-gritty details of our execution plan. This is where we'll outline our targets, geographic scope, and quality goals. Our goal here is to collect data from 100 schools, and we want to make sure it's high-quality stuff. Remember, Execution Plan is a key phrase here, so keep an eye out for it!

対象・目標 (Targets and Goals)

  • 対象地域 (Target Area): 関東1都6県（東京、神奈川、埼玉、千葉、茨城、栃木、群馬）(Kanto region: Tokyo, Kanagawa, Saitama, Chiba, Ibaraki, Tochigi, Gunma). We are focusing our efforts on the Kanto region, which includes the bustling metropolis of Tokyo and six surrounding prefectures. This diverse area will provide a rich dataset for our project.
  • 目標収集数 (Target Collection Count): 100校 (100 schools). Our primary goal is to gather data from 100 schools within the Kanto region. This number is a significant milestone and will greatly enhance our MVP app.
  • 品質目標 (Quality Target): A+B級80%以上 (80% or higher of A+B grade). Quality is paramount. We're aiming for at least 80% of the collected data to be of A+B grade quality. This ensures that our dataset is reliable and valuable.
  • 成功率目標 (Success Rate Target): 70%以上 (70% or higher). We are targeting a success rate of 70% or higher in our data collection efforts. This means we expect to successfully gather data from at least 70 out of every 100 attempts.

This prefecture-specific allocation helps us maintain a well-rounded dataset.
