Ultrasound treatments work similarly, using focused ultrasound energy to lift and tighten the skin. Laser treatments can also be effective for improving skin texture and firmness by promoting collagen remodeling.

For more significant sagging, minimally invasive procedures might be an option. These treatments involve slightly more downtime but often deliver more dramatic results. Thread lifts, for instance, involve inserting dissolvable threads under the skin to lift and support sagging tissues. This procedure can provide an immediate lifting effect and stimulate collagen production over time. Another option is microneedling, which involves creating tiny punctures in the skin to stimulate collagen and elastin production. When combined with radiofrequency, microneedling can be even more effective.

Procedures like tummy tucks (abdominoplasty) involve removing excess skin and fat and tightening the abdominal muscles.
