Interpolation Techniques

The advantage of splines is that they are relatively easy to compute and can provide a visually pleasing result.

However, spline interpolation doesn't guarantee that the resulting curve is a logarithmic spiral. It's an approximation technique that aims to create a smooth curve that passes through the given points.
