The Conversion Challenge: PLY (Gaussian Splats) to OFF (Triangles)

So, here's the million-dollar question: can we convert a PLY file containing Gaussian splats into a triangular OFF file? The short answer is: it's complicated. Therefore, it is important to carefully consider the trade-offs between accuracy, efficiency, and computational cost when converting Gaussian splats to triangular meshes.

Another challenge in this conversion process is dealing with the potential loss of detail and information. Gaussian splats, with their continuous representation, can capture fine details and subtle variations in the surface that may be difficult to represent accurately with triangles.
