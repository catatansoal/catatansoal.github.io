Figuring out the LCM is like finding a common ground for these numbers, a number that all of them can divide into evenly. So, what's the LCM of 7, 2, and 3? It's 42! Now, we're going to multiply every single term in the equation by this magic number, 42. This is a crucial step, guys, because it maintains the balance of the equation.
