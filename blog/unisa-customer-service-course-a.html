What You'll Learn in the Unisa Customer Service Course

The Unisa Customer Service Course is carefully structured to provide you with a well-rounded understanding of customer service principles and practices. The course also covers techniques for preventing escalation, documenting complaints, and using feedback to improve service quality. Finally, the Unisa Customer Service Course covers strategies for building long-term customer relationships. Customer retention is just as important as customer acquisition, and this module focuses on how to create lasting connections with your customers. You'll learn about loyalty programs, relationship marketing, and the importance of follow-up and ongoing communication.
