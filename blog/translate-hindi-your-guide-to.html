What is Self-Service Translation?

Self-service translation refers to the process of using online tools and software to translate content without the direct involvement of a human translator. These tools leverage machine translation (MT) technology, which uses algorithms and artificial intelligence to convert text from one language to another. Self-service translation in Hindi can range from simple word-for-word translations to more sophisticated interpretations that consider context and grammar. But what exactly does this mean for you? Self-service translation tools have become an indispensable asset in our globalized world. However, it's essential to understand the underlying technology to appreciate the benefits and limitations.
