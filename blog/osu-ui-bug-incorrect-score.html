The nitty-gritty of this bug is quite interesting. It's like the game is getting its wires crossed and pulling up the wrong data. The user who reported this bug rightly pointed out that it might be unintentional, but either way, it definitely feels wrong. Imagine the confusion if you're trying to beat your own score and the UI is showing you a number that doesn't even belong to you! It's a classic case of a small glitch causing a big headache.

Now, let's break down the technical side a bit. The issue seems to be localized to a specific area of the shaded region in the song select screen. This region is designed to provide you with a quick overview of your performance on each song, including your personal best score. When the bug occurs, the UI incorrectly pulls score data from another player, rather than your own. The important thing is that the displayed score doesn't match your actual personal best for that song. This discrepancy is what makes the bug so noticeable and frustrating.
