The Ultimate Homemade Dawn Powerwash Recipe

Okay, let‚Äôs get down to the nitty-gritty! This recipe is super simple and yields fantastic results. Grab your supplies, and let‚Äôs make some homemade Dawn Powerwash!

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup Dawn Dish Soap (the blue one is a classic!)
  • 2/3 cup Rubbing Alcohol (70% or higher)
  • 1 cup Water (distilled is best)

Instructions:

  1. Prep Your Bottle: Make sure your empty Dawn Powerwash bottle is clean and dry. Rinse it out thoroughly to remove any leftover residue. A clean bottle ensures the purity of your homemade Powerwash and prevents any unwanted contamination.
  2. Combine Ingredients: Using your measuring cups, pour the Dawn dish soap, rubbing alcohol, and water into the empty bottle. A funnel can be helpful here to prevent spills.
  3. Gently Mix: Screw the spray nozzle back onto the bottle and gently swirl the mixture to combine. Avoid shaking vigorously, as this can create excessive suds. Gentle swirling ensures the ingredients are thoroughly mixed without creating too many bubbles.
  4. Test and Adjust: Give it a test spray! If you feel like it's not quite as strong as you'd like, you can add a little more Dawn dish soap. If it's too strong, add a bit more water. Adjusting the recipe to your preferences is part of the fun of DIY!

Pro Tip: Label your bottle! This helps prevent any confusion, especially if you have other cleaning solutions in similar bottles. A clear label ensures everyone knows what's inside and avoids any accidental misuse.
