First off, let's clarify what a work profile actually is. In a legitimate setting, a work profile is a separate section on your phone designed to keep your personal and professional data distinct. Knowing the purpose and proper setup of work profiles helps you identify when something is amiss. Common Methods for Adding Work Profiles Without Consent
