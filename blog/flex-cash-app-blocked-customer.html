The truth is, there are several sophisticated methods that websites and applications use to detect and block connections, even those that appear to be anonymized. One common technique is deep packet inspection (DPI). DPI allows network administrators to examine the data content of packets as they pass through a network, not just the headers. This means that even if your traffic is encrypted, DPI can potentially identify patterns or signatures that indicate you're using a proxy or VPN. Another factor is IP address reputation. Geolocation blocking is another method employed to restrict access based on the user's geographic location. If your proxy server is located in a region that the website or application has blocked, your connection will be denied. Furthermore, browser fingerprinting techniques can be used to identify unique characteristics of your browser, such as the user-agent string, installed plugins, and screen resolution. These fingerprints can be used to track users across sessions, even if they're using different IP addresses. Timing analysis can also be employed to detect patterns in your network traffic that are indicative of proxy usage. For example, the latency introduced by routing your traffic through a proxy server may be noticeable and trigger a blockage. Why Your Secure Connection Might Still Be Blocked

This is a common issue with free or low-cost proxy services, as they tend to attract users with less-than-reputable intentions. Another factor to consider is deep packet inspection (DPI). DPI is a sophisticated technique that allows network administrators to examine the contents of data packets, not just the headers. This means that even if your traffic is encrypted, DPI can potentially identify patterns that suggest you're using a proxy or VPN. For example, certain VPN protocols have distinctive signatures that can be detected through DPI. Browser fingerprinting is another method used to track users online. Websites can collect information about your browser, such as your user-agent string, installed plugins, and screen resolution, to create a unique fingerprint. By analyzing the timing of your network traffic, websites may be able to detect patterns that are indicative of proxy usage. Finally, application-level detection is becoming increasingly common. Some applications, including the Flex Cash Loan App, may use advanced techniques to detect and block connections from proxies or VPNs.
