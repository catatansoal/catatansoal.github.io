Ominous Banner Stuck? Crafting Grid Bug In Refined Storage 2

Aug 6, 2025 by ADMIN 61 views

Hey guys, ever run into a super annoying bug that just won't let go? We've got one here with Refined Storage 2 that's making crafting a real headache. Let's dive into this Ominous Banner issue and see what's going on.

The Ominous Banner Bug in Refined Storage 2

So, here’s the deal: Players are reporting that when they place an Ominous Banner in the Crafting Grid, it gets stuck. Like, glued-in-place stuck. You can't remove it, relogging doesn't help, and it's just a pain in the blocky butt. This bug specifically affects the Ominous Banner in Refined Storage 2's Crafting Grid, making it impossible to remove once placed. This significantly disrupts gameplay, as the crafting grid becomes unusable until the issue is resolved. Identifying the root cause and implementing a fix are crucial for maintaining a smooth user experience. The issue has been observed in Minecraft version 1.21.1 with the Fabric mod loader, specifically when using Refined Storage version 2.0.0-beta.7. Further testing and debugging are necessary to pinpoint the exact mechanism causing this behavior and to ensure a stable and enjoyable crafting experience for players. It's frustrating, right? You're trying to craft something cool, and suddenly your grid is hostage to a banner. The developers need clear steps to reproduce the issue, so they can address it effectively. Let's walk through what players have tried and the environment details to give a complete picture.
