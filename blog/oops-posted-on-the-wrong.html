What to Do After You've Deleted: Damage Assessment and Follow-Up

So, you've deleted the post, and you've put some preventive measures in place. Great! But what happens next? Sometimes, deletion isn't the end of the story. Depending on the nature of the post and how quickly you caught it, you might need to do some damage assessment and follow up.

Keep an eye on your mentions and notifications on the platform where the mistake happened. See if anyone has commented on the post or shared it before you deleted it. Learn from the Mistake
