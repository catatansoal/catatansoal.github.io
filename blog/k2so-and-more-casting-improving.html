The core issue at hand is that the More Casting mod doesn't currently affect intermediates from other mods, such as those introduced by Krastorio 2 Spaced Out. What does this mean for you? Well, if you're using both mods, you might notice that the recipes for intermediate products like iron and steel from K2SO aren't being bypassed by the More Casting mod. To put it simply, the More Casting mod is designed to skip certain intermediate steps in crafting recipes, making it easier to produce complex items. However, it's not currently recognizing the intermediates introduced by K2SO. This means that items like iron beams, steel beams, and steel gears from K2SO still require you to go through all the intermediate crafting steps, even though the More Casting mod should, in theory, be bypassing them. This not only adds extra steps but also disrupts the flow of your factory, potentially slowing down your progress. Nobody wants that, right?
