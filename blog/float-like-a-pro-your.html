First, find a safe environment. Start in shallow water where you can comfortably stand and your feet can touch the bottom. To counter this, try to keep your arms out to the sides and gently kick your legs to stabilize yourself. Adjust your arm and leg positions until you feel balanced. Safety First: Essential Back Floating Guidelines

Now that we've covered the fun stuff, let's get serious for a moment. Safety should always be your top priority when you're in the water. Here are some essential guidelines to ensure you have a safe and enjoyable back-floating experience.
