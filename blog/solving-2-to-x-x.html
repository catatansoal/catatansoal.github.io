Rewriting sec(θ)tan(θ) as (1/cos(θ)) * (sin(θ)/cos(θ)) = sin(θ)/cos²(θ), we get:

I = 2^(2024) ∫[0 to π/2] [cos(θ) / (1 + sin(θ))]^{4049} [sin(θ) / cos²(θ)] dθ
