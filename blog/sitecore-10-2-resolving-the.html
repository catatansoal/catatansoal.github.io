Finding Alternatives: Where Did Everything Go?

Okay, so the assembly is gone, but what about the functionality it provided? Don't worry, the features haven't just disappeared into the digital void. They've likely been moved or refactored. Your job is to find the new homes for these functionalities. Here's what you need to do:

1. Analyze Your Code

5. Adapt and Migrate Your Code

  • Refactor Your Code: Adjust your code to use the new classes, methods, and properties. This may involve renaming variables, changing method calls, or even rewriting some parts of your code to align with the updated Sitecore architecture.
  • Test Thoroughly: After making changes, thoroughly test your solution to ensure that everything is working as expected. Make sure that your custom modules integrate correctly and that no functionality is broken.
