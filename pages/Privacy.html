Privacy

Aug 1, 2025

We consider the privacy of our visitors to be very important. This privacy policy document explains in detail the types of personal information that we collect and record, and how we use it.

Log Files

Like many other websites, our site uses log files. These files only log visitors to the site. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users' movement on the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information, are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Our site uses cookies to store information about visitors' preferences, to record user-specific information on which pages the site visitor accesses or visits, and to personalize or customize our web page content based on the type of browser the visitors use or other information that the visitors send via their browser.

DART DoubleClick Cookie

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site.

Third-party ad servers or ad networks that may be present on our website may use technology such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons in their advertisements and links that appear on our site, which are sent directly to your browser. They automatically receive your IP address. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by third-party ad networks on our site to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on our site. We do not have access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. Our privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such advertisers or other websites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through individual browser settings. For more information about managing cookies with specific browser types, please visit the appropriate browser's website.

Information about Children

We believe that it is extremely important to provide additional protection for children online. We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. Our site is not intentionally collecting any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian believes that our site has information from a child under the age of 13 in our database, please contact us immediately so that we can take the necessary steps to ensure that that information is removed from our records.

Online Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies only to activities on our website and its related services. It does not include websites linked to this site. You may find links to third-party websites on our website. We do not control the third-party websites and are not responsible for their content or their use of cookies.

Consent

By using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood our privacy policy and terms.
