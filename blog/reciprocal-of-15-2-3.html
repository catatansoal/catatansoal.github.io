Understanding Reciprocals: The Basics

So, what exactly is a reciprocal? In simple terms, the reciprocal of a number is just 1 divided by that number. For instance, the reciprocal of 2 is 1/2, and the reciprocal of 5 is 1/5. It's like flipping a fraction. If you start with a fraction a/b, its reciprocal is b/a. This concept is super important in math, especially when you're dealing with division. Dividing by a number is the same as multiplying by its reciprocal. Pretty neat, huh?
