Understanding the Microsoft Blue Screen of Death (BSoD)

A common culprit is a malfunctioning device driver. Drivers are the software that allows your operating system to communicate with your hardware, like your graphics card, printer, or even your mouse. Overclocking your CPU or GPU, which means running them at speeds higher than the manufacturer intended, can also lead to BSoDs. Key Improvements in Modern Windows
