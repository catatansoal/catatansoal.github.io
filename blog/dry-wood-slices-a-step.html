Tips for Preventing Cracks

Okay, so we've covered the main drying methods, but let's talk about some extra tips to prevent cracks in wood slices and ensure your wood slices stay in tip-top shape! Avoid exposing them to sudden changes in temperature or humidity, as this can cause them to dry unevenly and crack. A cool, dry, and well-ventilated space is ideal.
