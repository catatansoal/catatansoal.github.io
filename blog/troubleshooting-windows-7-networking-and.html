Ah, Microsoft Excel, the spreadsheet software that's both a blessing and a curse. It's an incredibly powerful tool, but let's be honest, it can also be a bit of a beast to tame. The key is to double-check, triple-check, and maybe even quadruple-check your formulas. Look out for common errors like incorrect cell references (is that A1 or A2?), missing parentheses, and those pesky division-by-zero errors. Another common issue is formatting.
